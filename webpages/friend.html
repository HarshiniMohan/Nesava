ABOUT US

Handloom products are textiles and fabric-based items that are meticulously crafted using traditional manual weaving techniques, often carried out on small-scale, non-mechanized looms. These products are the result of skilled artisans manipulating threads by hand to create intricate patterns, designs, and textures. Handloom weaving is a labor-intensive process that involves the careful interlacing of warp and weft threads to form the desired fabric.
